# Overview
The Joint Research Centre (JRC) is the European Commission's in-house science and knowledge service. It supports EU and national policymakers with independent scientific evidence and technical support.

# Strategic Role
The JRC is embedded within Pillar II's 'Global Challenges and European Industrial Competitiveness' architecture. It provides the scientific underpinning for EU policy across all clusters.

# Horizon Europe Relevance
- Activities within Pillar II, alongside the six thematic clusters
- Subject to separate work programmes not entirely covered by the Programme Guide
- Provides cross-cutting scientific support to all HE clusters
