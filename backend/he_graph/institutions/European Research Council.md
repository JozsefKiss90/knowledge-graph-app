# Overview
The European Research Council (ERC) supports frontier research projects defined and driven by top researchers themselves. It operates under Pillar I ('Excellent Science') of [[Horizon Europe]].

# Strategic Role
The ERC is the EU's flagship instrument for investigator-driven, bottom-up frontier research across all fields. It awards grants based solely on scientific excellence, without thematic restrictions.

# Horizon Europe Relevance
- Part of Pillar I 'Excellent Science'
- Separate work programme and evaluation processes
- [[Open Science]] practices not included in ERC evaluation criteria (exception to general HE rules)
- Open peer review provisions do not apply to ERC calls
- International organisations with headquarters in a Member State or associated country deemed established there for ERC purposes
