# Overview
The European Institute of Innovation and Technology (EIT) develops the overall European innovation landscape by fostering the integration of the knowledge triangle of education, research, and innovation.

# Strategic Role
The EIT operates through Knowledge and Innovation Communities (KICs), which are institutionalised [[European Partnerships]]. KICs address skills shortages and were already established under Horizon 2020. Key partners include higher education institutions, research organisations, companies, and other stakeholders.

# Horizon Europe Relevance
- Part of Pillar III 'Innovative Europe' (though not part of the Specific Programme)
- KICs are institutionalised European partnerships
- Separate work programme not covered by the HE Programme Guide
