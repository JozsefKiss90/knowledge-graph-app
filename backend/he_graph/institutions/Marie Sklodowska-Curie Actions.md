# Overview
Marie Sklodowska-Curie Actions (MSCA) fund fellowships for postdoctoral researchers, doctoral training networks, and exchanges for researchers. They operate under Pillar I ('Excellent Science') of [[Horizon Europe]].

# Strategic Role
MSCA is the EU's primary instrument for researcher mobility and career development. In the 2026-2027 WP, MSCA allocates about EUR 50 million to improve long-term fellowships, ensure postdoctoral career stability, and offer relocation incentives.

# Horizon Europe Relevance
- Part of Pillar I 'Excellent Science'
- Separate work programme and call guidance
- Key vehicle for the [[Choose Europe for Science]] initiative
- Links MSCA grants to competitions for long-term positions in universities and research organisations
- Natural persons in Russia, Belarus, or non-government-controlled territories of Ukraine may participate in MSCA if not subject to EU restrictive measures
