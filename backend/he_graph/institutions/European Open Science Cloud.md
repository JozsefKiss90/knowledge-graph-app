# Overview
The European Open Science Cloud (EOSC) aims to deploy and consolidate an open, trusted virtual environment to enable circa 2 million European researchers to store, share, process, analyse, and reuse research digital objects including data, publications, and software across disciplines and borders.

# Strategic Role
EOSC is implemented as a European co-programmed partnership for the period 2021-2030. It brings together institutional, national, and European initiatives to deploy a European Research Data Commons where data are Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable (FAIR).

# Horizon Europe Relevance
- The Research Infrastructures part of the 2026-2027 WP contributes to enriching EOSC with high-quality, FAIR research data and scientific tools
- Certain work programmes require use of trusted repositories federated in EOSC
- Data deposited in EOSC must support FAIR principles
- Supports cross-disciplinary science cases
- Part of the European Strategy for Data
