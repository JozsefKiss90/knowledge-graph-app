# Overview
The European Innovation Council (EIC) aims to make Europe a frontrunner in market-creating innovation. It operates under Pillar III ('Innovative Europe') of [[Horizon Europe]].

# Strategic Role
The EIC identifies and supports breakthrough technologies and innovations with potential to scale up internationally. Its Accelerator instrument provides blended finance (grants + equity) to high-risk, high-potential startups and SMEs.

# Horizon Europe Relevance
- Part of Pillar III 'Innovative Europe'
- Separate work programme
- Flagship [[Gender Equality]] measures: 40% target for women-led companies pitching, 50% women target for EIC advisory structures
- Dedicated initiative for women-led deep-tech start-ups
- Supports [[Innovation Procurement]] through SME links
- Supports [[Choose Europe for Science]] by creating favourable conditions for technology-driven companies
