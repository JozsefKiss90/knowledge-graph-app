# Overview
The European Innovation Ecosystems (EIE) programme enhances interconnected innovation ecosystems across the EU. It operates alongside Pillar III of [[Horizon Europe]].

# Strategic Role
The EIE contributes to boosting Europe's innovation capabilities, notably in the deep tech sector, and improving the commercialisation of research. It fosters synergies among diverse innovation actors for green and digital transition, competitiveness, and social cohesion. It supports startups and scaleups to strengthen economic resilience and [[Open Strategic Autonomy]].

# Horizon Europe Relevance
- The European Startup and Scaleup Hubs initiative creates a network connecting Europe's leading startups
- Part of the Startup and Scaleup Strategy
- Supports the [[Choose Europe for Science]] initiative by creating conditions for technology-driven companies
