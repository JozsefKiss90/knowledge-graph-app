# Overview
'AI in Science' is a horizontal call in the [[Horizon Europe]] 2026-2027 Work Programme aimed at fostering trustworthy AI solutions to tackle significant societal and industrial challenges. It contributes to the 'Resource for AI Science in Europe' (RAISE) pilot, as announced in the European Strategy for Artificial Intelligence in Science.

# Strategic Role
The call supports safe, responsible, ethical, and transparent AI applications across key sectors: advanced materials, agriculture, environment and food, and broader digital transition goals. It seeks to enhance Europe's R&I leadership and strengthen its strategic position in the global AI landscape.

# Budget and Topics
- Indicative budget: EUR 90 million
- Open topics on: **thematic networks of excellence for AI in science**, **automated scientific discovery**, and **RAISE doctoral networks for AI in science**
- In parallel, at least EUR 775 million invested in AI in science through the rest of the WP
- Overall AI investment estimated at EUR 2,023 million for 2026-2027
- Estimated 35.8% of funds across clusters/RI/WPERA/EIE/Missions/NEB/Horizontal contribute to AI

# Policy Context
Aligned with the AI Continent Action Plan's commitment to accelerating AI development and deployment. Promotes AI 'made in Europe' — from research labs to marketplace.
