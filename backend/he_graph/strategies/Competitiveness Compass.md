# Overview
The Competitiveness Compass is an EU strategic framework that expands on the Commission's Political Guidelines for 2024-2029. It requires significant contributions from R&I to meet its objectives.

# Horizon Europe Relevance
The 2026-2027 work programme is fully aligned with the Competitiveness Compass, contributing substantially to all three strategic orientations: green transition, digital transition, and a more resilient/competitive/inclusive/democratic Europe.
