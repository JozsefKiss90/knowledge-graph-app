# Overview
The second Horizon Europe Strategic Plan (2025-2027) sets overarching strategic orientations for EU R&I investments in the final period of the framework programme. It defines 32 expected impacts structured across six clusters, with three interlinked key strategic orientations.

# Three Key Strategic Orientations
1. **The green transition** — climate neutrality, biodiversity, sustainable resources
2. **The digital transition** — AI, digital technologies, digital sovereignty
3. **A more resilient, competitive, inclusive, and democratic Europe** — security, social cohesion, democratic values
