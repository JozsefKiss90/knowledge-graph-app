# Overview
The European Green Deal is the EU's overarching strategy for achieving climate neutrality by 2050. Its Communication COM(2019) 640 introduced the 'do no harm' principle that evolved into the [[Do No Significant Harm]] framework under the EU Taxonomy.

# Horizon Europe Relevance
- Horizon Europe's first strategic orientation ('green transition') is directly aligned with the Green Deal
- 35% of HE funding allocated to climate objectives
- EU Missions support Green Deal goals (cancer, climate adaptation, oceans, smart cities, soil)
- [[Clean Industrial Deal]] extends Green Deal into industrial policy
- The Green Deal provides the policy foundation for Clusters 5 and 6
