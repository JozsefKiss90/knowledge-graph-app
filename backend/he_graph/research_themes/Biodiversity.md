# Overview
Biodiversity protection and restoration is a growing priority in [[Horizon Europe]], with at least 10% of the 2025-2027 budget dedicated to biodiversity-related topics (EUR 1,661 million, 15.7% of relevant budget in 2026-2027).

# Horizon Europe Relevance
- One of six environmental objectives under [[Do No Significant Harm]]
- [[Cluster 6 Food Bioeconomy Natural Resources Agriculture and Environment]] addresses main drivers of biodiversity loss
- EU Mission 'A Soil Deal for Europe' supports biodiversity through soil health
- EU Mission 'Restore our Ocean and Waters by 2030' targets aquatic ecosystems
- Aligned with the Kunming-Montreal Global Biodiversity Framework and the Nature Restoration Regulation
