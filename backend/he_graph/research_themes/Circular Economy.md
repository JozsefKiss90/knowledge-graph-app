# Overview
The EU's shift to a circular economy is integral to the [[Horizon Europe]] 2026-2027 work programme, with systemic changes across sectors such as construction and textiles. It is one of six environmental objectives under [[Do No Significant Harm]].

# Horizon Europe Relevance
- Cross-cluster actions promoting innovative, market-ready solutions for circularity
- [[Clean Industrial Deal]] horizontal call includes circularity in energy-intensive industries
- Circular Cities and Regions Initiative under the CID
- [[Cluster 6 Food Bioeconomy Natural Resources Agriculture and Environment]] fosters bio-based products and circular value chains
