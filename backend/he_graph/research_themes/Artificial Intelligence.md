# Overview
Artificial Intelligence is a central research theme in [[Horizon Europe]], receiving substantial investment across all programme parts. The EU is committed to developing safe, ethical, and trustworthy AI applications 'made in Europe' that drive innovation from research labs to marketplace.

# Horizon Europe Relevance
- Overall AI investment estimated at EUR 2,023 million for 2026-2027
- Estimated 35.8% of funds across clusters, RI, WPERA, EIE, Missions, NEB, and Horizontal Activities contribute to AI
- 33% of all HE funds contribute to the digital transition
- EUR 13 billion minimum allocated to core digital technologies over HE lifetime
- Horizontal call [[AI in Science]] (EUR 90M) + EUR 775M additional through the WP
- AI ethics covered under [[Ethics and Integrity]] review (including AI-specific ethics guidelines)
