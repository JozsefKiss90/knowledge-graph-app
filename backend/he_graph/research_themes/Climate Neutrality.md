# Overview
Climate neutrality by 2050 is a central objective of the EU, pursued through the [[European Green Deal]] and reflected across [[Horizon Europe]]. At least 35% of HE funding is allocated to climate objectives.

# Horizon Europe Relevance
- EUR 4,903 million (46.3% of Pillar II + related parts budget) to climate action in 2026-2027
- [[Cluster 5 Climate Energy and Mobility]] contributes 89.7% of its budget to climate action
- EU Mission on Climate-Neutral and Smart Cities: 100 cities by 2030
- EU Mission on Adaptation to Climate Change: 150 regions by 2030
- [[Do No Significant Harm]] principle protects climate mitigation and adaptation objectives
