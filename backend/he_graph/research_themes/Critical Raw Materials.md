# Overview
Critical Raw Materials (CRM) are materials of high economic importance with significant supply risk. In [[Horizon Europe]], EUR 634 million is allocated to CRM-related topics in the 2026-2027 work programme.

# Horizon Europe Relevance
- Research on [[Advanced Materials]] and alternatives to rare earth
- Reducing strategic dependencies in critical supply chains
- Aligned with [[Open Strategic Autonomy]] objectives
- Supports the [[Clean Industrial Deal]] through clean technology supply chains
