# Overview
Cluster 4 'Digital, Industry and Space' is a central cluster under Pillar II of [[Horizon Europe]], focused on ensuring a substantial and coordinated contribution by R&I to the [[Clean Industrial Deal]], promoting emerging enabling technologies such as [[AI in Science|AI]], and achieving [[Open Strategic Autonomy]] in space-based infrastructure, services, applications, and data.

# Strategic Role
This cluster is the primary vehicle for the EU's digital and industrial policy ambitions within Horizon Europe. It operates in alignment with the horizontal calls on the [[Clean Industrial Deal]] and [[AI in Science]], and helps build synergies and joint activities between [[European Partnerships]].

# Policy Context
- Aligned with the [[Competitiveness Compass]] for industrial competitiveness
- Supports the [[Clean Industrial Deal]] through R&I on decarbonisation and clean technologies
- Advances [[Open Strategic Autonomy]] in critical technologies
- EUR 634 million allocated to topics related to [[Critical Raw Materials]]
- Supports research on [[Advanced Materials]] and alternatives to rare earth

# Horizon Europe Relevance
- Budget contribution to climate action: 22.6% (EUR 437 million) in the 2026-2027 WP
- Major contributor to AI investment (estimated EUR 2,023 million across all HE in 2026-2027)
- Invests in [[Quantum Technologies]], energy technologies, and advanced manufacturing
- EUR 13 billion minimum allocated to core digital technologies over HE lifetime

# Evolution Across Work Programmes
The 2026-2027 WP marks a significant shift toward cross-cluster horizontal calls (Clean Industrial Deal, AI in Science) that deeply involve Cluster 4. The cluster also co-finances the Clean Industrial Deal call under Horizontal Activities. Growing emphasis on [[Open Strategic Autonomy]] and reduction of strategic dependencies.

# Implications for Proposal Design
- Participation restrictions may apply for actions related to EU strategic assets
- [[Open Strategic Autonomy]] framing is critical for many topics
- Integration of [[AI in Science]] approaches increasingly valued
- [[Lump Sum Funding]] applies to 50% of call budget
- Many topics use [[Two-Stage Calls]] with anonymised first-stage evaluation
