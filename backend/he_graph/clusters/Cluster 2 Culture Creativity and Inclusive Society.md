# Overview
Cluster 2 'Culture, Creativity and Inclusive Society' operates under Pillar II of [[Horizon Europe]]. It mobilises social sciences and humanities (SSH) and interdisciplinary R&I to foster a democratic culture of participation and inclusivity, promote values of equality, freedom, respect for human dignity and human rights, and cultural pluralism.

# Strategic Role
This cluster ensures EU values remain relevant in addressing contemporary challenges and in balancing technological progress with societal impact. It contributes to a more inclusive and equitable Europe where innovation benefits all.

# Policy Context
Aligned with the EU's commitment to democracy, fundamental rights, and the fight against exclusion and discrimination. The 2026-2027 WP introduces a new [[European Partnerships|European partnership on social transformations and resilience]], the last of nine new partnerships announced in the second Horizon Europe strategic plan (2025-2027).

# Horizon Europe Relevance
- Budget contribution to climate action: 10.0% (EUR 64 million) in the 2026-2027 WP
- Central cluster for [[SSH Integration]] and [[Gender Equality]] research (gender studies and intersectional research)
- The new European partnership on social transformations and resilience funds R&I on: future of work, modernisation of social protection, education and skills, fair transition to climate neutrality

# Evolution Across Work Programmes
The 2026-2027 WP marks the launch of the European partnership on social transformations and resilience — the final new partnership of the second strategic plan. Increased focus on balancing technological progress with societal impact, particularly regarding AI and digital exclusion.
