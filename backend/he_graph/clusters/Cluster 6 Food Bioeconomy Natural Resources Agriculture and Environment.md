# Overview
Cluster 6 'Food, Bioeconomy, Natural Resources, Agriculture and Environment' is part of Pillar II of [[Horizon Europe]]. It is pivotal for sustaining quality of life, from food security to water and nature. Actions support implementation of the common agricultural policy, the vision for agriculture and food, and the Nature Restoration Regulation.

# Strategic Role
The cluster addresses the main drivers of [[Biodiversity]] loss, pollution, water-related challenges (supporting the European Water Resilience Strategy), and helps protect and restore ocean health (European Ocean Pact). It pursues climate adaptation and mitigation by helping land- and water-based sectors transition to [[Climate Neutrality]] by 2050.

# Evolution Across Work Programmes
The 2026-2027 WP increases emphasis on circularity (in alignment with the EU's circular economy shift), bio-based value chains, Nature Restoration Regulation implementation, and the Biodiversity 10% spending target introduced in the second strategic plan (2025-2027).
