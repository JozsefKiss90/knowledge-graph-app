# Horizon Europe Cluster Interactions

This synthesis maps the inter-cluster relationships and convergence points across Pillar II of [[Horizon Europe]].

---

## Cross-Cluster Convergence Themes

### 1. Artificial Intelligence
All clusters engage with AI: health diagnostics (CL1), cultural AI ethics (CL2), security AI (CL3), core AI tech (CL4), smart energy/mobility (CL5), precision agriculture (CL6). Estimated EUR 2,023M investment.

### 2. Climate Action
All clusters contribute to the 35% climate spending target, from 10% (CL2) to 89.7% (CL5).

### 3. Clean Industrial Deal
The horizontal call connects CL4 (digital/industry) and CL5 (energy/mobility) with EUR 540M, while CL6 contributes circularity perspectives.

### 4. Social Sciences and Humanities
While CL2 is the primary SSH cluster, [[SSH Integration]] is mandated across all clusters. SSH-flagged topics appear in every cluster.

### 5. Biodiversity
Primarily CL6, but with contributions from CL1 (environmental health), CL5 (land-use change), and missions.

## Horizontal Call Architecture

The 2026-2027 WP introduces horizontal calls that formally break cluster silos:

```
Horizontal Activities
├── Clean Industrial Deal (EUR 540M)
│   ├── CL4 co-financing
│   ├── CL5 co-financing
│   └── Cross-sectoral industry topics
├── AI in Science (EUR 90M)
│   ├── Cross-cluster AI applications
│   ├── RAISE doctoral networks
│   └── Thematic networks of excellence
└── New European Bauhaus
    └── Cross-cluster sustainability + arts
```

## Institutional Bridges
- **[[European Partnerships]]**: operate across clusters (e.g., automotive partnerships link CL4+CL5)
- **[[EU Missions]]**: by design cross-cluster and transdisciplinary
- **[[Joint Research Centre]]**: provides scientific support to all clusters
- **[[European Open Science Cloud]]**: data infrastructure for all clusters
