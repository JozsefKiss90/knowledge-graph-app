# AI Ecosystem in Horizon Europe

This synthesis maps the full AI investment and governance landscape within [[Horizon Europe]].

---

## AI Investment Architecture (2026-2027)

| Component | Budget |
|-----------|--------|
| [[AI in Science]] horizontal call | EUR 90 million |
| AI in science through rest of WP | EUR 775+ million |
| Total AI development investment | EUR 2,023 million |
| Digital transition (all HE, estimated) | 33% of total funds |
| AI-contributing share of clusters/RI/WPERA/EIE/Missions/NEB/Horizontal | 35.8% (EUR 3,780M) |
| Core digital technologies (HE lifetime minimum) | EUR 13 billion |

## AI Across Programme Architecture

### Horizontal Level
- **[[AI in Science]]** call: thematic networks of excellence, automated scientific discovery, RAISE doctoral networks
- RAISE pilot (Resource for AI Science in Europe)

### Cluster Level
- **[[Cluster 4 Digital Industry and Space]]**: primary implementing cluster for AI technologies
- **[[Cluster 1 Health]]**: AI for healthcare, biotechnology, drug discovery
- **[[Cluster 5 Climate Energy and Mobility]]**: AI for energy optimisation, smart mobility
- **[[Cluster 6 Food Bioeconomy Natural Resources Agriculture and Environment]]**: AI for precision agriculture
- **[[Cluster 3 Civil Security for Society]]**: AI for cybersecurity, threat detection

### Pillar I
- **[[Research Infrastructures]]**: AI-enabled research tools
- **[[European Open Science Cloud]]**: AI-ready data infrastructure

### Cross-Cutting
- **[[Ethics and Integrity]]**: AI ethics included in ethics review scope
- **[[Open Science]]**: AI models and algorithms as research outputs

## AI Governance Framework
- AI ethics is explicitly part of the Ethics Appraisal Procedure
- Guidelines on ethics by design/operational use for AI
- EU AI Act compliance expected for applicable projects
- Trustworthiness, safety, and transparency as core requirements
- Gender and intersectional bias in AI recognised as an ethical concern

## Policy Alignment
- **AI Continent Action Plan**: accelerating development and deployment
- **European Strategy for AI in Science**: RAISE pilot
- **[[Open Strategic Autonomy]]**: AI sovereignty, 'made in Europe'
- **[[Clean Industrial Deal]]**: AI for industrial decarbonisation
- **[[Competitiveness Compass]]**: AI for economic competitiveness

## Strategic Observations
1. AI is the single largest thematic investment in the 2026-2027 WP
2. The horizontal call model treats AI as a cross-domain enabler, not a standalone discipline
3. The RAISE pilot signals a shift toward AI as research infrastructure
4. Ethics governance is embedded early (ethics review) rather than post-hoc
5. Doctoral networks indicate long-term workforce investment
6. AI investment spans all three strategic orientations (green, digital, resilience)
