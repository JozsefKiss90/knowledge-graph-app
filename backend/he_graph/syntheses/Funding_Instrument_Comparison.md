# Funding Instrument Comparison

This synthesis compares the key funding instruments and mechanisms in [[Horizon Europe]].

---

## Action Types

| Action Type | Purpose | Funding Rate | Key Characteristics |
|-------------|---------|-------------|---------------------|
| **Research and Innovation Actions (RIA)** | Generate new knowledge / explore feasibility | 100% | Evaluated on Excellence, Impact, Implementation |
| **Innovation Actions (IA)** | Prototyping, testing, piloting, market replication | 70% (100% for non-profit) | Closer to market; China entities excluded |
| **Coordination and Support Actions (CSA)** | Networking, coordination, policy support | 100% | No research component; supports innovation procurement feasibility |
| **Programme Co-fund Actions** | Top-up funding for national/regional programmes | Variable | Used for MSCA COFUND, ERA-NET |
| **PCP Actions** | Pre-commercial procurement of R&D services | Variable | Buyer groups (min. 2 countries); no commercial deployment |
| **PPI Actions** | Public procurement of innovative solutions | Variable | Buyer groups; near-market solutions |

## Evaluation Criteria

| Criterion | RIA/IA Weight | Key Elements |
|-----------|---------------|--------------|
| **Excellence** | Highest | Methodology, [[Open Science]], state of the art, gender dimension |
| **Impact** | High | Outcomes, pathway to impact, D&E plan, policy alignment |
| **Implementation** | Moderate | Work plan, consortium, resources, management |

## Simplification Mechanisms (2026-2027)

| Mechanism | Coverage | Purpose |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| [[Lump Sum Funding]] | 50% of call budget | Simplified financial management |
| [[Two-Stage Calls]] | 41 topics | Reduced submission effort |
| Anonymised evaluation | Many first-stage proposals | Reduce bias |
| Less prescriptive topics | Across Pillar II | More applicant flexibility |
| Reduced page limits | Standard templates | Lower preparation burden |
| Open topics | Expanded in Pillar II | Pathways and methods open-ended |

## Special Funding Features

### Horizontal Calls
- **[[Clean Industrial Deal]]**: EUR 540M, industry-led, cross-sectoral
- **[[AI in Science]]**: EUR 90M, cross-cluster AI applications

### Newcomer-Friendly Topics
- Specifically designed for SMEs, start-ups, civil society, small public administrations
- Fast Track to Research and Innovation Scheme
- More financial support to third parties topics

### Key Budget Allocations (2026-2027)
- Total WP budget: >EUR 14 billion
- Climate objectives: EUR 4,903M (46.3% of relevant budget)
- Biodiversity: EUR 1,661M (15.7%)
- Digital transition: 33% of all funds
- AI investment: EUR 2,023M
- Knowledge valorisation: EUR 870M
- Critical raw materials: EUR 634M
- ERA Chairs: EUR 240M
- Clean Industrial Deal horizontal: EUR 540M
- AI in Science horizontal: EUR 90M

## Cross-Cutting Obligations

All funded projects must address:
- [[Open Science]] (mandatory practices)
- [[Ethics and Integrity]] (ethics self-assessment)
- [[Gender Equality]] (GEP eligibility + gender dimension)
- [[Dissemination and Exploitation]] (D&E plan)
- [[Do No Significant Harm]] (environmental assessment)
- Security self-assessment
