# Overview
The [[Horizon Europe]] 2026-2027 work programme includes 41 call topics with two-stage evaluation. Applicants submit a shorter summary proposal first; if successful, they submit a full proposal. Many short proposals are evaluated blindly through anonymised applications.

# Benefits
- Reduced submission effort for unsuccessful applicants
- Shorter time to grant for first-stage proposals
- Reduced potential risk of evaluation bias through anonymisation
- Simplified evaluation processes

- [[Lump Sum Funding]] — complementary simplification
