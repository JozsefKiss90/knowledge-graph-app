# Overview
Lump sum funding is an increasingly used financing mechanism in [[Horizon Europe]] that simplifies financial management and reduces administrative burden for participants. In the 2026-2027 work programme, 50% of the call budget will be implemented through lump sums, particularly for topics with grants below EUR 10 million.
