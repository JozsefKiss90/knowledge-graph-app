# Overview
The New European Bauhaus (NEB) is a cross-cluster initiative in [[Horizon Europe]] that leverages the power of inclusion, sustainability, and arts and culture in R&I to advance the [[European Green Deal]] and clean transition in neighbourhoods and communities.

# Horizon Europe Relevance
- Fosters R&I connecting green transformation, social inclusion, local democracy, and circular/regenerative approaches to the built environment
- Develops innovative funding and new business models for neighbourhood transformation
- Budget contributes to climate action (part of the 68.7% Horizontal Activities contribution)
