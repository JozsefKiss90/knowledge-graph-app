# Overview
Innovation procurement uses public procurement to drive innovation from the demand side, speeding up development and adoption of innovative solutions for public services and societal challenges. It is a cross-cutting topic of importance for all pillars of [[Horizon Europe]].

# Two Types
- **Public Procurement of Innovative Solutions (PPI)**: for near-market solutions
- **Pre-Commercial Procurement (PCP)**: for new R&D solutions; does not include commercial deployment

# Support Mechanisms
- **Coordination and Support Actions (CSA)**: networking and feasibility
- **PCP or PPI Actions**: co-finance procurement costs for buyer groups (minimum 2 public procurers from different EU/associated countries)
