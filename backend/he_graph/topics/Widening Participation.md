# Overview
'Widening Participation and Strengthening the European Research Area' is a cross-cutting part of [[Horizon Europe]] that supports the ERA's goals by addressing R&I fragmentation, reducing geographical disparities, and enhancing R&I capacity. It targets 3% of GDP for research and development.

# Key Initiatives (2026-2027)
- **ERA Chairs**: EUR 240 million to attract top scientists to widening countries; now includes recruiting leading research managers
- **'Talent ecosystems for attractive early research careers'**: promotes new European framework for research careers
- Supports the [[Choose Europe for Science]] initiative
- Increased budget compared to previous WPs
