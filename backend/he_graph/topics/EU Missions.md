# Overview
EU Missions are commitments to solve some of the greatest challenges facing the world. They operate as a portfolio of actions — research projects, policy measures, legislative initiatives — to achieve measurable, time-bound goals. They are an integral part of [[Horizon Europe]].

# Five EU Missions
1. **Adaptation to Climate Change**: helping at least 150 European regions and communities become climate resilient by 2030
2. **Cancer**: improving the lives of more than 3 million people by 2030 through prevention and cure
3. **Restore our Ocean and Waters by 2030**: protecting and restoring aquatic ecosystems, eliminating pollution, making the blue economy circular and carbon-neutral
4. **Climate-Neutral and Smart Cities**: delivering 100 climate-neutral and smart cities by 2030
5. **A Soil Deal for Europe**: setting up 100 living labs and lighthouses to lead the transition towards healthy soils by 2030

# 2026-2027 Work Programme Specifics
- Enhance local and regional climate risk preparedness
- Provide solutions to restore EU waters
- Establish Climate City Contracts with 100 cities
- Create a unified framework for soil-health monitoring
- Support cancer prevention through clinical trials and biomarker-guided medicines
- Mission integration award and Mission engagement award launched
