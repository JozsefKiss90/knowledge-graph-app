# Overview
Research Infrastructures operate under Pillar I ('Excellent Science') of [[Horizon Europe]], investing in world-class research facilities. The 2026-2027 WP aims to strengthen them to support all stages of the innovation process — from basic research to market use.

# Horizon Europe Relevance
- Nearly EUR 50 million for [[Choose Europe for Science]] contributions
- Enriches [[European Open Science Cloud]] with FAIR research data and scientific tools
- Supports cross-disciplinary science cases
- Aligned with the European strategy on research and technology infrastructures
